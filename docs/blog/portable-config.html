Portable config

The parts of Sussurro that become truly yours — your dictionary, snippets and per-app styles — travel between machines as a single JSON file.

3 min read · Feature

The longer you use Sussurro, the more it learns about you: the names it spells right, the snippets you've saved, the tone rules per app. Portable config lets you carry all of that to a new laptop — or share it across your own machines — without setting it up twice.

What's included

  • Your personal dictionary
  • Your voice snippets
  • Your per-app tone styles

Export & import

Export writes a single JSON file. On another machine, import it — and here's the nice part: import merges rather than overwrites, skipping duplicates. So you can combine setups from two machines without losing entries or creating copies.

Backup, too

Because it's one portable file, an export doubles as a backup of your personalization. Keep a copy and a fresh install is a minute away from feeling like home.

It's the same philosophy as the rest of Sussurro: your data is yours, in an open format, on your disk — easy to move, easy to keep.

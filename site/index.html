How it works

Press. Speak. Release. Done.

A local pipeline runs the moment you let go of the key — no round-trip, no waiting on a server.

Capture

Your microphone is recorded and resampled to 16 kHz — locally, only while the key is held.

Transcribe

whisper.cpp on your GPU (Vulkan / Metal), or NVIDIA Parakeet on CPU — ~10× faster without a GPU.

Clean up

A small local LLM removes fillers, fixes punctuation and matches the tone of the app you're in.

Inject

The result is pasted where your cursor is — in any app — and your clipboard is restored.

Features

More than transcription.

Everything Wispr Flow does — plus a few things it can't, because Sussurro runs the model itself.

AI cleanup, 4 levels

None to High. Removes “um”, fixes grammar and punctuation — with graceful fallback to the raw transcript if the model is off.

Translate as you speak

Dictate in one language, get clean text in another — even with cleanup off. A local trick the cloud tools can't match.

Per-app tone

Casual with emoji in Slack, formal in email. Rules adapt the cleanup — and the output language — to the app in focus.

Streaming typing

Words appear while you talk — live, or sentence-by-sentence with each one cleaned before it's typed.

Two STT engines

Whisper for any language on the GPU, or NVIDIA Parakeet — one CPU-optimized model that auto-detects 25 European languages.

Command mode

Select any text, hold a key, and speak an instruction — “make it shorter”, “fix the grammar”. The LLM rewrites it in place.

Searchable history

Every dictation, stored locally as JSONL. Search, re-clean, translate, edit, export to Markdown or JSON. Retention is yours to set.

Self-learning dictionary

Fix a word once in your history and Sussurro remembers it — your names and jargon spelled right, automatically.

Local API

An opt-in loopback endpoint: clean text, transcribe files or query your history from any script. Off by default.

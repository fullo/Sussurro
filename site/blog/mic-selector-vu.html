Microphone selector & VU meter

The wrong input device is the most common reason dictation “doesn't work”. Sussurro lets you pick the mic and see its level live, so you know it's listening.

3 min read · Feature

Between a laptop mic, a headset, a webcam and a USB interface, your system's “default” input isn't always the one you're speaking into. Sussurro makes the choice explicit and gives you instant visual confirmation.

Pick your device

Choose the capture device in Settings. If that device is later unplugged, Sussurro gracefully falls back to the system default, so a disconnected headset never leaves you with silence.

See it working

Next to the selector is a Test button with a live input-level bar — a VU meter. Speak, and watch it move. The same level bar appears while you record, so you always have a signal that Sussurro is hearing you.

A quick sanity check

Before an important dictation, tap Test and say a word. If the bar jumps, you're set. If it doesn't, you've caught a muted or wrong mic before it cost you a sentence.

Getting clean audio in is half of good transcription — the other half is choosing the right engine for your hardware.

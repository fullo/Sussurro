Whisper mode: dictate quietly

A shared office, a sleeping baby, a late night. Whisper mode tunes the audio path so Sussurro hears you even when you're barely making a sound.

3 min read · Feature

Not every moment is right for talking at full volume. Whisper mode (the feature, not the engine) lets you dictate in an actual whisper by making two adjustments to how your audio is captured.

What it changes

  • 3× microphone gain — quiet speech is amplified before it reaches the recognizer.
  • A lower silence gate — the threshold that decides “this is speech, not background” drops, so soft sounds still register.
When to switch it on

Open-plan offices, libraries, shared rooms, or any time you'd rather not be overheard. Toggle it off again for normal-volume dictation, where the standard gate does a better job of ignoring background noise.

Pair it with the overlay

Because you're speaking softly, the on-screen recording overlay and the VU meter are especially handy — a glance confirms Sussurro is picking up your whisper before you commit to a whole sentence.
